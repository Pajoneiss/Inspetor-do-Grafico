## 2. A Day in the Life (Pro Routine)
What is the real routine? (Not the Instagram one).

*   **08:00:** Wake up. Coffee. Don't open chart yet. Check news (Investing.com). Is there Payroll? War?
*   **09:00:** Open Daily Chart (D1). Macro trend? Bullish. Ok, today I only look for buys.
*   **09:30 (NY Open):** Chaos starts. He doesn't trade. He watches liquidity traps (Judas Swing).
*   **10:30:** Dust settles. Setup appears. Price hit Order Block and printed a Hammer.
*   **Execution:** Calculates risk (1% of account). Configures Bot. Clicks "Start".
*   **11:00 - 16:00:** He goes to live. Gym, Reading, Family. Bot handles the trade.
*   **18:00:** Returns. Trade hit target? Great. Stopped out? Fine. Logs in Journal. Closes screen.

Zero emotion. Zero screaming. Just cold, constant execution.
