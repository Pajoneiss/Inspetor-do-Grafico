## 1. The Math of Survival

### The Winrate Fallacy
Beginners seek "90% accuracy". Not needed.
You can get rich being right only 40% of the time. How? **Risk:Reward (R:R).**

*   **Trader A:** 90% accuracy. Wins $10, loses $100. (Inverse R:R). Goes broke.
*   **Trader B:** 40% accuracy. Wins $300, loses $100. (1:3 R:R).
    *   10 trades: 6 Losses (-$600) + 4 Wins (+$1200) = **+$600 Net Profit**.

The Bot seeks asymmetric bets. Minimum 1:2 R:R.

### Position Sizing
Rule #1: **Never get kicked out of the game.**
*   Never risk more than 1-2% of capital per trade.
*   If you lose 50%, you need 100% profit just to break even. Protect your capital like your life.

## 2. Psychological Demons

### FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out)
Price explodes up. Brain screams "BUY NOW!". You buy top. Price corrects. You panic sell bottom.
*   **Antidote:** Wait for pullback. Market always gives a second chance. If it leaves without you, let it go.

### Revenge Trading
You take a loss. You get angry. "Market won't take my money!". You double down (leverage) to make it back fast. You lose again. You blow the account.
*   **Antidote:** Accept loss as "cost of doing business". Walk away.

### Confirmation Bias
You want to buy Bitcoin. You ignore 10 sell signals and focus on the 1 buy signal.
*   **Antidote:** Ask: "If I wasn't positioned, would I enter now?". Be the devil's advocate.

## 3. The Flow State
Best traders feel neither euphoria nor pain. They operate in calm focus.
*   They have a plan **before** clicking.
*   They know exactly where to exit taking profit or loss **before** entering.
*   They accept the random outcome of *one* trade, focusing on the statistical outcome of *100* trades.
