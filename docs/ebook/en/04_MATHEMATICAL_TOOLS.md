## 1. Fibonacci: The Signature of God
Leonardo Fibonacci discovered in the 13th century a numerical sequence (1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8...) that governs everything from galaxy spirals to rabbit reproduction. And, incredibly, the human mind reacts to these proportions in the market.

### Retracement (Where to enter the Pullback)
After a leg up (Impulse), price breathes (Retracement). How far does it go?
*   **0.382 (38.2%):** Shallow correction. Indicates VERY STRONG trend. Buyers are eager.
*   **0.50 (50%):** Midpoint (Equilibrium). Psychological zone, though not a real Fib number.
*   **0.618 (61.8% - Golden Ratio):** The most powerful support in the universe. If price hits here and rejects, it's textbook entry.
*   **0.786 (78.6%):** The "Last Frontier". Often coincides with an Order Block (OTE - Optimal Trade Entry).

**Trap:** Buying *blindly* at 61.8%.
**Correction:** Wait for price to hit 61.8% **AND** print a Hammer or Engulfing. Fibonacci is the zone, Candle is the trigger.

### Extension (Where to take Profit)
Where will price stop after breaking the top?
*   **1.618:** Classic euphoria target.
*   **2.618:** Blow-off top (insane bubbles).

## 2. Moving Averages: Price Memory
Price is erratic. Average is smooth.
*   **SMA (Simple) vs EMA (Exponential):** Simple treats today's price same as 100 days ago. Exponential weighs today more. Inspector prefers EMA as it reacts faster.
*   **Death Cross:** 50 MA crosses below 200 MA. Classic Long Bear Market sign.
*   **Golden Cross:** The opposite. Bull Run sign.

*   **Practical Tip:** Use EMA 200 as "Trend Filter".
    *   Price above EMA 200? Only look for Buys.
    *   Price below? Only look for Sells.
    *   This prevents you from catching a falling knife.

## 3. RSI (Relative Strength Index): The Thermometer
Measures speed of price change. 0 to 100.
*   **Overbought (>70):** Price rose too fast. Might be "expensive".
*   **Oversold (<30):** Price fell too fast. Might be "cheap".

### THE BIG MYTH (Retail Fatal Error)
"RSI hit 70, I will sell!"
**Wrong.** In a strong bull market, RSI can stay above 70 for **weeks**. Price keeps doubling while RSI sits there "overbought". Selling just because of RSI 70 is asking to be run over by the train.

**The Real Secret: DIVERGENCES**
*   **Regular Bearish:** Price makes HIGHER HIGH. RSI makes LOWER HIGH.
    *   *Translation:* Price went up, but engine "force" decreased. Fuel empty. Reversal imminent.
*   **Regular Bullish:** Price makes LOWER LOW. RSI makes HIGHER LOW.
    *   *Translation:* Sellers tired.

## 4. VWAP (Volume Weighted Average Price): The Big Player Price
Unlike normal averages, VWAP considers **Volume**.
It shows the real average price paid by all participants today.

*   **Why it matters?** Hedge Funds have bonus targets based on VWAP. If they buy below daily VWAP, they get bonuses. If above, they get fired.
*   **The Effect:** When price returns to VWAP, institutional bots defend that line with claws and teeth. It is a super strong Intraday dynamic support/resistance.

---

## 5. ADX: The Trend Detector (Noise Filter)
Most traders lose money trading trends in a sideways market.
ADX tells you if a trend **exists**.
*   **ADX < 20:** Sideways Market (Range). Trend indicators will fail. Use oscillators (RSI) and trade range tops/bottoms.
*   **ADX > 25:** Strong Trend calling. Stop fading it. Hold the trade longer.
